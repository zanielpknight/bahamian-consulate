Mrs. Alveta Adderley-Knight Consul General

In 2024, the Consulate General underwent a comprehensive upgrade to its physical premises, digital infrastructure, and service delivery framework. Under the leadership of Consul General Adderley-Knight, the mission has expanded its operational capacity to better serve the growing Bahamian diaspora in Jamaica and strengthen the long-standing diplomatic relationship between the two Caribbean nations.

The expanded mandate includes enhanced citizen welfare programs, streamlined document processing, emergency preparedness coordination, and deepened engagement with Jamaican government agencies on matters of mutual interest, including trade facilitation, cultural exchange, and regional security cooperation through CARICOM.
