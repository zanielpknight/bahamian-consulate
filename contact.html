Emergency Assistance

For after-hours emergencies involving Bahamian nationals in Jamaica, please contact:

  • Consulate Emergency WhatsApp: +1 (876) 436-8768
  • Ministry of Foreign Affairs (Nassau): +1 (242) 356-5956
  • MOFA After-Hours Emergency: +1 (242) 827-3106

For life-threatening emergencies, always call Jamaica's emergency services at 119 (police) or 110 (fire/ambulance) first.

For Jamaican Nationals

Jamaican nationals seeking information about travelling to The Bahamas can visit our Visa & Travel page. No visa is required for stays up to three months.

For Jamaican consular services, please contact the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Foreign Trade of Jamaica.
